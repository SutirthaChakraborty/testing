Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring meal plans for offices. These can include daily lunches, team breakfasts, or specific days of the week. We offer discounts for recurring orders.

We provide clear labeling for all dishes including allergen information and dietary specifications (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.). When setting up corporate accounts, we collect dietary information for your team members and ensure appropriate options are always available.

For corporate catering, we require a minimum of 10 people for one-time orders. For recurring meal plans, we can accommodate smaller teams of 5 or more. Special arrangements can be made for executive meetings with fewer participants.

For regular office meals, we recommend ordering at least 24 hours in advance. For larger corporate events or conferences, 3-5 days notice is preferred. Last-minute orders may be accommodated based on our schedule and capacity.

Yes, we offer monthly invoicing for corporate clients with recurring orders. We can also work with your company's procurement system and provide detailed invoices with cost centers or department codes as needed.

We understand that business needs can change quickly. We do our best to accommodate last-minute changes or additions when possible. Please notify us as soon as you're aware of any changes needed to your order.
