Daily Fresh Indian Meals Delivered to Your Door

"Ditch the takeaways, embrace the dabba-wala way!"

Our signature Tiffin Service brings the authentic taste of India right to your doorstep in Dublin. Inspired by Mumbai's legendary dabba-wala system, we deliver freshly prepared, home-style Indian meals daily or weekly according to your preference.

Each tiffin is prepared with love, using traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients. It's the perfect solution for busy professionals, students, and families who crave nutritious, homemade Indian food without the hassle of cooking.

Benefits of Our Tiffin Service:

  • Fresh, home-style cooking
  • Balanced, nutritious meals
  • Rotating menu with diverse regional dishes
  • Flexible subscription options
  • No wastage - perfect portion sizes

How Our Tiffin Service Works

Simple, convenient, and delicious - bringing homestyle Indian cooking to your table has never been easier

1. Subscribe

Choose your preferred tiffin plan, select delivery days, and mention any dietary preferences or restrictions.

Simple as chai!

2. We Cook

Our chefs prepare fresh meals daily using authentic recipes and the finest ingredients, just like home cooking.

Swad apne ghar ka!

3. We Deliver

Your tiffin arrives at your doorstep within your selected time slot, hot and ready to enjoy!

No more bother!

4. Return & Repeat

We collect the previous day's tiffin boxes and deliver fresh meals. Eco-friendly and zero waste!

Jugaad at its best!

Frequently Asked Questions

We currently deliver to most areas within Dublin city and some surrounding areas. During the subscription process, you can check if delivery is available at your address. We're continuously expanding our delivery zones!

We take allergies and dietary restrictions very seriously. During the subscription process, you can specify any allergies or preferences. We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specialized menu options. Our kitchen takes great care to avoid cross-contamination.

Absolutely! You can pause, skip, or modify your tiffin deliveries with 48 hours' notice. Simply log into your account or contact our customer service team, and we'll take care of it.

We use high-quality, eco-friendly stainless steel tiffin containers that keep your food hot and fresh. Our delivery team ensures prompt delivery within your chosen time slot. The tiffin boxes are collected the next day when your new meal is delivered.

Yes, you can order additional tiffin boxes for guests by notifying us at least 24 hours in advance. These will be charged at your regular subscription rate or slightly higher for one-off additions.
